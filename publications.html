On the local behavior of chains on strongly pseudoconvex hypersurfaces in C3

Authors: N. Bakaev, S. Ban, H. El Bouz, G. Della Sala, A. Hussein, J. Moussa
Proceedings of the American Mathematical Society (2025)
JournalComplex AnalysisGeometry
Abstract

We study chains on strongly pseudoconvex hypersurfaces in C3 via Fefferman’s metric and the Chern–Moser normal form, analyzing local behavior and invariants under biholomorphisms.
